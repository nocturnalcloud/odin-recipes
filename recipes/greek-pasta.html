Greek Pasta with Tomatoes and White Beans

With cannellini beans, tomatoes, and feta cheese, this warm pasta dish is an easy, tasty summer dinner full of Greek flavors.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces penne pasta
  • 2 (14.5 ounce) cans Italian-style diced tomatoes
  • 1 (19 ounce) can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
  • 10 ounces fresh spinach, washed and chopped
  • ½ cup crumbled feta cheese

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Stir in penne and return to a boil. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, 10 to 12 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, combine tomatoes and beans in a large non-stick skillet; bring to a boil over medium high heat, then reduce heat to medium-low and simmer 10 minutes. Add spinach; cook until wilted, 2 minutes, stirring constantly.
  3. Serve sauce over pasta, and sprinkle with feta.
